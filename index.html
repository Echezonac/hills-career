Hills Academy is an annual technology program organized by the Hills tech community. it was founded in the year 2019, with the aim of empowering Africans with technological training, mentorship, and employment. Hills tech community is currently made up of 120 members across the globe. Our members are technological experts with several years of experience with the interest to give back to society by mentoring and building a proficient tech expert in fields like web application development, mobile app development, block-chain development, CAD/BIM, Graphic specialist, Ethical Hacking e.t.c.

What is Work 'N' Class ?

Work 'N' Class is an free annual tech skill acquisition program organised by Hills Tech Community to empower Africans and promote technology in Africa

Who are eligible to participate ?

Any one with interest and passion to learn any technological skills. Our curriculum starts from the basics to Advance topic and projects.

How much is the cost of the program ?

Work 'N' Class is totally free but we encourage interest candidate to contribute a little token to the sustainance of the program. The donation are use to purchase data for your mentor, Humamitarian works, and to elevate those in need around your community.

What do i need to start the program ?

You need your laptop(preferable)/tablet/phone, a telegram account, an active email address and your commitment.

How is the classes organise?

After your registration, a form will be sent to your email to fill out, after that your mentor will contact you when the program start. you are expected to have created a telegram account with the number you registered with. Your class is an on-demand video, each topic will be recorded by your mentor and sent to you with your assignment and projects. You are expected to be in contact with your mentor daily, giving him feedback or asking him questions.

When will i be having classes ?

Classes are Mondays, Wednesdays and Thursdays. You will be given project during weekend to submit on Sundays.

When is the registration deadline for this program ?

Registration ends on 30th November 2021 and your classes starts on 1st December 2021.

Can i be expel from this program?

Yes!! you can. if you display bad attitude towards your learning consistently.
